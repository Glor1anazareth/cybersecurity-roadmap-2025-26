# Introduction to Hacking, Types of Hacking and Lab Setup

## Course Overview:
-Four main sections:
  1.  Network Hacking
  2.  Gaining Access
  3.  Post Exploitation
  4.  Website Hacking

## What is Hacking?
  - Gaining unauthorized access to systems
  - Used ethically to secure systems

## Types of Hackers
- **Black Hat:** Malicious,illegal
- **White Hat:** Ethical, authorized
- **Gray Hat:** Inbetween, sometimes unauthorized but non-malicious

## Why Learn Hacking?
- Understand attacks to **protect systems**
- High industry demand
- Develop foundational cybersecurity skills

## Lab Setup
- Setup a **Virtual Lab** for safe testing
- Use **isolated environment** (VMs)
- Only perform attacks **inside labs**
- Prepare for hands-on exercises in next sessions
